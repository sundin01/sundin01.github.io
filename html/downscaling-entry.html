Downscaling and Application Intro

For many practical applications, reanalysis data sets are too coarse. A further step of downscaling is required to reach resolutions of kilometers. From such local weather information, data products for further application can then be generated. Experience these steps by downscaling a reanalysis and deriving a targeted hydrological product.

Learning Outcome

Learning outcome: Students can name two different types of approaches for downscaling. They are able to apply a simple downscaling technique. Students can generate a targeted climatological product from a time series.
