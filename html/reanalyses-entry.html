Reanalysis Intro

Historical measurements provide a very limited view of the weather situation of a given day. How can we reconstruct the full state of the atmosphere? The answer is: By combining the measurements with numerical weather prediction models. Learn how data assimilation works and how to interpret the resulting reanalysis data sets.

Learning Outcome

Students understand the principles of data assimilation, they are able to define a distance measure, they are able to apply data assimilation for a very simple example.
