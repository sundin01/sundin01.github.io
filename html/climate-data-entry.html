Climate Data Intro

Weather reconstructions rely on historical measurements. Before we can work with historical data, however, the data must be digitised, processed, quality controlled, and possibly homogenized. Learn how to bring data into a useful form and undertake these four steps yourself.

Learning Outcome

Students are able to process raw data and convert it into a comparable form. They are able to detect gross errors. They understand the concept of break detection and homogenisation and are able to apply a simple homogenisation method.
